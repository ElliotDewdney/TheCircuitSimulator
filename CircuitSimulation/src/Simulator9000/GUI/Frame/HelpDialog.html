How to use : The Circuit Designer

How to use the circuit editor:

  • To start your circuit select File from the drop down then new to open the editor,
  • Build your circuit by dragging circuit components from the toolbar screen and place them on the Canvas,
  • Draw wires to connect your elements by clicking on the connection point that are either side of the components, or one of the junctions located on a wire,
  • Once in wiring mode click to change direction or click on a connection point/wire junction to end the wire there,
  • To delete a junction and the surrounding wires you right click on it,
  • To delete a component you double right click on it, and to open the inspector window double left click,
  • Use the inspector window to adjust the values of the properties of a component
  • Keep adding elements and wires to your circuit until your circuit is complete

Click the Simulate from Actions in the drop down, to enter the simulation mode.

Tips for simulation mode:

  • Click Action from the drop down menu then Settings then simulation mode, to change how the data is shown onscreen
  • During simulation values might not be 100% percent accurate, shorting cells will often cause problems
  • If you wish to change your circuit revert to Circuit editor mode by Selecting Action then Stop Simulation

Other Things of Note:

  • To get the image of the circuit you have made go to File then Export then select either copy to clipboard or Export as file
  • To Change the theme go to Action from the drop down menu then Settings then themes mode, to change background colour
